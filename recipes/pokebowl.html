What is a pokebowl

A poke bowl (original spelling 'poke bowl', pronounced “poh-KAY bowl”) is a Hawaiian dish that has its roots deeply rooted in Japanese cuisine.

“Poke” means “cut into pieces” and the name refers to the slices or cubes of raw fish that are served in a bowl along with rice, dressing, vegetables and seasonings.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • protein such a chicken on beef
  • Bacon
  • lettuce or pickle and other veggies
