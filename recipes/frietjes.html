Frietjes

What are frietjes

Unlike their wimpy, vegetable-oil-fried American progeny, Belgian frites are always thick-cut, made with floury Bintje potatoes, and double-cooked in beef tallow.

They're first pre-fried at a lower heat and then briefly deep-fried at a much higher heat just before serving.

Ingredients

  • floury Bintje potatoes
  • beef tallow
  • little bit of salt
